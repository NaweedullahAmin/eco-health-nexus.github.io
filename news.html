July 2025 Publication

New Research on Zinc Oxide Nanoparticles Published in Environmental Toxicology & Chemistry by Oxford University Press

Our latest research on the effects of salinity in mitigating ZnO nanoparticle toxicity in Javanese Medaka embryos has been published in Environmental Toxicology and Chemistry. This study provides important insights into environmental risk assessment of nanoparticles.

Five New Research Projects Launched Focusing on Kabul Environmental Issues

Our research group has initiated five new interdisciplinary studies addressing critical environmental and public health challenges in Kabul. The projects cover water safety, medical waste management, plastic pollution, air quality, and fish consumption patterns.

Our work on nanoplastics toxicity and fate will be presented at the National Conference on Environmental Sustainability in Kabul. The presentation will highlight the dual role of environment and particle characteristics in shaping nanoplastic toxicity.
