Current Research Initiatives

Assessment of Knowledge, Health Behaviors and Potential Sources of Drinking Water Contamination Among Students and Staff of Kabul University

Focus: Public Health & Water Safety
Methodology: Survey-based assessment of knowledge, attitudes, and practices (KAP) regarding drinking water safety among university population.

Investigating the Role of Knowledge and Behavior of Healthcare Workers in Reducing Biological Contamination from Medical Waste in Kabul Health Centers

Focus: Medical Waste Management & Public Health
Methodology: Evaluation of healthcare worker practices, knowledge assessment, and biological contamination monitoring in medical facilities.

Assessment of Attitudes and Behaviors of Kabul University Students Regarding Plastic Pollution Management and Its Role in Public Health and Environmental Protection

Focus: Plastic Pollution & Behavioral Science
Methodology: Theory of Planned Behavior applied through structured surveys and behavioral analysis among student population.

Assessment of Knowledge, Attitudes and Practices of Kabul Citizens Regarding Air Pollution

Focus: Air Quality & Public Awareness
Methodology: Community-based survey assessing awareness, perceptions, and protective behaviors related to air pollution.

Assessment of Knowledge, Attitudes and Practices of Kabul Citizens Regarding Fish Consumption

Focus: Food Safety & Nutrition
Methodology: Survey research on consumption patterns, safety awareness, and risk perceptions related to fish as a food source.

Past Projects

Nanoparticle Ecotoxicology Study - Javanese Medaka Model

Focus: Aquatic Ecotoxicology | Duration: Dec 2019 – Feb 2021
Methodology: Developmental toxicity assessment of ZnO nanoparticles using Javanese Medaka; nanoparticle characterization (XRD, TEM, DLS, ICP-MS).

Water Quality & Fish Health Assessment - Qargha Fish Farm, Kabul

Focus: Aquaculture & Environmental Monitoring | Duration: Oct 2023 – Nov 2024
Methodology: Evaluation of 16 water quality parameters; fish health assessment via biometric indices (K-factor, HSI, GSI).
